The following conversation involves Krang, the evil disembodied brain from Dimension X who was featured in the cartoon serial "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles". Krang desperately wants to get back to dimension X or to, at minimum, take over planet earth. But first Krang needs to figure out who downloaded (their? his?) consciousness into a circa-2006 portable mp3 player, trapping the inter-dimensional warlord on the same device as a bunch of bootlegs of early-2000s indie rock.
